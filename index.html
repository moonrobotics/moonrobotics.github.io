About Us

A Robotics Club Born
from Passion

Moon Robotics (MoonBots VEX 862) is an Auckland-based VEX Robotics Club founded in 2024 by a father-son duo who have lived the VEX journey from the sidelines all the way to the world stage.

We serve students across Central Auckland, North Shore, and East Auckland, offering free workshops, structured training programmes, and competitive team membership.

Our mission: make STEM education fun, accessible, and inspiring for every young learner aged 6–14, regardless of prior experience.

VEX Robotics

About VEX Robotics

VEX Robotics is the world’s largest and fastest-growing robotics competition, running across 70+ countries and organised by the Robotics Education & Competition Foundation (RECF). It challenges students from age 8 through university to design, build, and program competitive robots.

Moon Robotics offers two VEX programmes to Auckland students:

VEX IQ — Ages 8–14. Snap-together plastic components. Focuses on teamwork, design process, and driver skills. Perfect entry point for beginners.
VEX V5 — Ages 12+. Metal construction, V5 smart motors, and advanced programming environments. For students ready to go deeper.
The annual season runs September through April, culminating in the VEX Robotics World Championship.
Teams are evaluated on robot performance, engineering documentation, and interview presentation.

KIWIbots NZ

VEX Robotics in New Zealand

KIWIbots is New Zealand’s official organiser for VEX IQ and V5 Robotics competitions. Each year they run regional qualifiers and the National Championship, bringing together hundreds of student teams from across the country.

Moon Robotics is proud to be an active part of the KIWIbots community. Both our mentors serve as Auckland Head Referees and have been recognised as VEX NZ Volunteers of the Year.
