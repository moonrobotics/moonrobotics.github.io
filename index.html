About Us

A Robotics Club Born from Passion

Moon Robotics (MoonBots VEX 862) is an Auckland-based VEX Robotics Club founded in 2024 by a father‑son duo.

We serve students across all Auckland area.

Our mission: make STEM education fun, accessible and inspiring for every learner aged 6–15.

Accolades

Moon Robotics mentors have earned top honours in New Zealand's VEX ecosystem — including two consecutive VEX NZ Volunteer of the Year awards, national championship titles, and representation at VEX World Championships.

How Does it Work?

Rangatahi that go through our programmes gain invaluable experience in:

  • Creative problem solving
  • Teamwork (especially under pressure!)
  • Engineering design
  • Working with complex electronics
  • Hands-on building and material work
  • Coding and IT Skills
  • Cross-cultural relationship building
