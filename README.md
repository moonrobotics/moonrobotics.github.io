# 🌙 Moon Robotics Club — Auckland VEX Robotics & STEM Education

**Moon Robotics (MoonBots VEX 862)** is Auckland's premier VEX IQ robotics club and STEM education centre for students aged 6–15.
Founded in 2024 by a VEX World Champion and his father, we offer free workshops, structured training programmes, and competitive VEX teams across 5 Auckland locations.

🌐 **Website:** [https://moonrobotics.github.io/](https://moonrobotics.github.io/)
📧 **Contact:** moonbots862@gmail.com

## 🏆 About Moon Robotics

Moon Robotics (MoonBots VEX 862) is an **Auckland-based VEX Robotics Club** founded by Jason Huang and his son Evan Huang — a 4× VEX World Championship competitor and VEX V5 Engineering Challenge **World Champion 2022**.

Our mission is to make **STEM education** fun, accessible, and inspiring for every young learner in Auckland, New Zealand.

We are proud to serve students across **Central Auckland, North Shore, East Auckland, West Auckland, and South Auckland**.

## 🎓 Programs & Courses

### Free Workshops
- Held at schools, libraries, and community events across all Auckland training centres
- **FREE** · Ages 6–15 · No experience needed
- Locations: Avondale Library, Albany Library, Howick Library and more

### Training Program (5 / 10 Sessions)
- Structured beginner curriculum covering robot building, programming, and teamwork
- Suitable for ages 6–15, no prior experience required

### Competition Teams — VEX IQ & VEX V5
- Join a full VEX annual season team
- Compete at Auckland Scrimmages → NZ National Championship → VEX World Championship

### Club Incubation
- Support for starting new VEX robotics clubs across New Zealand
- Mentoring, resources, and coaching provided

---

## 🤖 VEX Robotics Competitions

### VEX IQ Robotics Competition
- **Ages:** 6–15
- Plastic snap-together robot kits
- Focus on teamwork, programming, and engineering design
- **2026–27 Season Game:** Level Up

**Season Pathway:**
`Build & Program (May–Jun)` → `Auckland Scrimmages (Jul–Dec)` → `NZ Nationals (Dec)` → `World Championship (Apr · St. Louis)`

### VEX V5 Robotics Competition
- **Ages:** 14–18
- Metal parts, advanced programming, full engineering notebook
- `Scrimmages (Jul–Feb)` → `NZ Nationals (Feb)` → `World Championship (Apr · St. Louis)`
